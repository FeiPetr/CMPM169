Experiment 7: Data Visualization & Networks

Imitate

I was a bit stumped on how I wanted to approach this assignment. I’m really into cuckoos and other brood parasites and decided I wanted to do something with data regarding them, so I went online and downloaded a csv file of cuckoo egg data. For context, cuckoos lay their eggs in a host bird’s nest and make them raise their children. This practice is known as brood parasitism.

After looking into how tables worked in p5js, I successfully got my egg data displaying.

Integrate

I wasn’t super satisfied with the text display, so I used Plotly to create a scatterplot of data. I had to use ChatGPT to help me learn Plotly, as there isn’t much documentation on it for p5js.

I made a great visualization of cuckoo egg breath and length in general, but it doesn’t tell us why an egg might be a certain length or breadth. Since we have information on the host nests of the eggs, I decided to color code each point by host species.

I found this information really interesting and exciting! There seems to be a noticeable correlation between host species of the cuckoo and the size of the egg. Wrens seem to host smaller cuckoo eggs, while tree pipits seem to host larger cuckoos. This is really interesting because the actual species of cuckoo is all the same. It’s hard to make definitive theories without more data points, but my amateur guess would be that cuckoo mothers who lay smaller eggs tend to lay them in nests with birds that also have smaller eggs and vice versa as to avoid having their eggs rejected.
